# 2022 NHK Robokon 
This project is to fly paper airplanes from a robot.

## What I did make?
- Control and Sensing program(ROS2)
- IMU sensor app V2 (iOS)
- Robot controller app (iOS)
- Motor ESC contorller progam (STM32)
- Motor ESC (KiCAD)
- Robotic mechanical design (Fusion360)
and more.
